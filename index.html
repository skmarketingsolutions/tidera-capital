What We Are
A private financing brokerage with access to lenders your bank will never offer.

Tidera Capital exists in the space between borrower and lender. We don't hold deposits, underwrite risk, or originate loans. We do something more valuable: we know exactly which lender in our network wants your deal — and we put you in front of them with a curated package that closes.

What We Are Not
We are not a bank. We are not a lender. We do not compete with your lender.

Every lender in our network is independently vetted. We are compensated by lenders, not borrowers — meaning our service costs you nothing. We match based on fit, not commission incentives. Our reputation depends on placing you correctly the first time.

The Process

How we match
buyers with capital.

Three defined steps. One advisor, start to finish. No handoffs, no black boxes, no surprises.

01
Submit Your Financing Profile

Complete our intake form — five minutes, no commitment, no hard credit pull. We capture the information lenders actually need to evaluate your deal: vessel type, purchase price, estimated down payment, credit score range, and any unique circumstances (LLC ownership, foreign national status, self-employment, etc.).

Takes approximately five minutes to complete
No hard credit pull — soft inquiry only if required for initial qualification
Your information is not shared until lender fit is confirmed
A dedicated advisor is assigned immediately upon submission
02
We Build Your Package & Shop the Network

Your advisor builds a curated submission package — the kind of presentation that arrives at a lender's desk pre-qualified, organized, and positioned for approval. We then match your profile against our active lender network, identifying programs that fit your vessel type, loan amount, and credit profile. We don't blast your file. We place it precisely.

Advisor prepares a lender-ready summary of your deal
We match against 47+ active lender programs — not just the easy ones
Programs are selected based on fit, rate, and speed — not referral fees
Initial match returned within 24 hours of completed submission
03
Review Options. Select Terms. Close.

We present your lender options side-by-side: rate, term, LTV, amortization schedule, prepayment terms, and any program-specific requirements. Your advisor walks through each option with you, answers every question, and helps you evaluate the full picture — not just the rate. Once you select, we coordinate the documentation, manage the lender relationship, and drive toward closing.

Options presented in plain language — not lender boilerplate
Side-by-side comparison of all relevant loan terms
Advisor manages all lender communication and document coordination
Most transactions fund within 10–21 days of lender selection
⏱
Typical timeline: Profile submitted → Initial lender match in <24 hours → Term sheet in 3–5 business days → Documentation & underwriting 7–14 days → Funding in 10–21 days from lender selection. Complex transactions (commercial, multi-vessel, superyacht) may require additional time.
