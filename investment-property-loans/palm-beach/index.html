Frequently Asked Questions — Investment Property Loans in Palm Beach

What is a DSCR loan and can your network match me with one in Palm Beach, FL?
A DSCR (Debt-Service Coverage Ratio) loan qualifies you based on the property's rental income rather than your personal income. Our network includes lenders offering DSCR programs for investment properties throughout Palm Beach, FL and South Florida.
Can I finance a short-term rental (Airbnb) through your lender network in Palm Beach, FL?
Yes. Our network includes lenders who recognize short-term rental income for qualification purposes in markets like Palm Beach. These programs are available for properly zoned properties that comply with local STR regulations.
Do you connect investors with bridge loan lenders in Palm Beach, FL?
Absolutely. Bridge loan lenders in our network provide short-term financing for Palm Beach, FL investors who need to close quickly, are acquiring distressed properties, or need to bridge to a permanent loan.
Can I finance a portfolio of investment properties through Tidera Capital?
Portfolio loans that bundle multiple properties are available through select lenders in our network. This can be an efficient structure for investors with multiple Palm Beach, FL properties who want simplified financing.
