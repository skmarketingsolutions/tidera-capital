Our Story

Miami's Independent Financing Brokerage for Marine and Real Estate

Tidera Capital was founded with a single mission: give high-net-worth buyers in South Florida access to the financing programs they deserve — programs that the major banks and online lenders simply do not offer.

As an independent broker, Tidera Capital does not directly lend, fund, or underwrite loans. Instead, we have built an exclusive network of 50+ marine and real estate lenders who compete for your business. Our job is to match you with the right lender for your specific situation — and to do it within 24 hours.

Our team brings deep expertise in South Florida's marine and real estate markets. We understand the difference between financing a 45-foot express cruiser at Dinner Key Marina and a 120-foot superyacht through a European-flagged structure. We understand the difference between a DSCR rental property in Brickell and a waterfront estate in Palm Beach.

This expertise means we do not waste your time. We match you with lenders who actually do deals like yours — not lenders who will turn you down after weeks of paperwork.

Tidera Capital LLC is a financing consultant and broker. We connect borrowers with lenders in our network. We do not directly fund, originate, underwrite, or issue loans or mortgages. What Makes Tidera Capital Different

We Work for You, Not the Lender

As an independent broker, our loyalty is to the borrower. We search our entire network to find the program that best fits your needs — not the lender who pays us the most.
